About Theta Tau at UCLA
Theta Tau is a co-ed professional engineering fraternity that unites similar, yet unique individuals in a brotherly bond. Together, we navigate the turbulent ups-and-downs of university life as a STEM student: tackling difficult classes, forming life-long friendships, developing career-oriented skills, going on late night adventures, and more. Come and see if Theta Tau is the right community for you!
