About Upsilon Pi Epsilon
Upsilon Pi Epsilon is the International Honor Society for the Computing and Information Disciplines. UPE aims to maximize the personal and professional growth of students in the field of Computer Science. Through mentorship, corporate events, tutoring, and outreach, we intend to support students' growth in technology and computer science.
